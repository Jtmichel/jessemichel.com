Digital Strategy + Civic Duty.

10 lessons learned in difficult times (earned the hard way but free for you).

  1. The simpler solution is the more powerful one.
  2. Commit to fewer things and do them well.
  3. Trust your instincts.
  4. Don't prolongue painful conversations.
  5. Be kind to others, and to yourself.
  6. Ask. Don't assume.
  7. Be transparent, demand transparency.
  8. Be accountable, demand accountability.
  9. Break complex problems down to the smallest pieces.
  10. Listen and understand.
